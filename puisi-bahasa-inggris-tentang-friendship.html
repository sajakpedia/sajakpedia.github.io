10. buatlah thesis dan buat argumen minimal 5 dengan judul the importance of building friendship anywhere dalam bahas Inggris. tolong bantu butuh cepet​


Thesis Statement: Building and maintaining friendships is a vital aspect of human life that transcends geographical boundaries, cultures, and circumstances. Friendships formed anywhere can significantly impact an individual's personal growth, mental well-being, and overall happiness.

Argument 1: Emotional Support

- Friendships provide a crucial source of emotional support. Whether you're in your hometown or a foreign country, friends can offer a listening ear, empathy, and comfort during challenging times.

 Argument 2: Expanding Horizons

- Friendships forged in diverse environments expose individuals to new perspectives, ideas, and cultures. These interactions broaden one's horizons and enhance their understanding of the world, fostering personal growth.

Argument 3: Professional Networking

- Friendships extend beyond the personal sphere and can also benefit one's career. Building relationships anywhere can lead to valuable professional connections and opportunities.

Argument 4: Coping with Change

- Life often involves relocation and change. Having friends in different places can ease the transition process, reduce feelings of isolation, and provide a sense of belonging and stability.

Argument 5: Celebrating Diversity

- Friendships formed anywhere celebrate diversity and promote inclusivity. They exemplify the idea that human connections can transcend borders and bring people from various backgrounds together.
