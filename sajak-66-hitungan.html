21. Hitung n jika C(n-1,2)=66


(n - 1)C2 = 66

(n - 1) ! / ((n - 1 - 2) ! . 2 !) = 66

(n - 1) . (n - 2) . (n - 3) ! / ((n - 3) ! . 2) = 66

(n - 1) . (n - 2) = 66 . 2

n² - 3n + 2 = 132

n² - 3n + 2 - 132 = 0

n² - 3n - 130 = 0

(n - 13) . (n + 10) = 0

n + 10 = 0

n = -10 (tidak memenuhi karena n negatif)


n - 13 = 0

n = 13 (memenuhi karena positif)


Maka, n = 13
