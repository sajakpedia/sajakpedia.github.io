14. Dialog bahasa inggris tetang covid. Ada yg bisa bantu kak?


Bagaimana contoh dialognya dalam bahasa Inggris? Simak contoh dialog tentang Covid-19 berikut ini:

Contoh dialog 1

Dino: Hi, my sister. Are you doing your assignment?

Dina: Yes. This is so boring. I want to go to school again.

Dino: Let’s pray that this pandemic will end soon.

Dina: Is the virus really dangerous?

Dino: Yes, Dina. WHO even declares Covid-19 a pandemic.

Dina: Really? That’s why we have to stay at home?

Dino: Yes. Because we can easily get infected if we make contact with other people outside. Who knows that someone get infected? That’s why we must stay at home and avoid direct contact with people, except our family at home.

Dina: It’s so sad. I miss my friends.

Dino: Yes, I miss my friends, too. But we must obey the rules and keep ourselves safe.

Dina: How long do you think before we can live normal again?

Dino: I don't know, Dina. Nobody knows for sure. That's why we have to keep ourselves safe, so the pandemic can ends soon.

Dina: Okay. Mom said we also have to exercise and eat healthy foods, right?

Dino: Absolutely! You’re smart, Dina.

Terjemahan

Dino: Hai, adikku. Apakah kamu sedang mengerjakan tugasmu?

Dina: Ya. Ini sangat membosankan. Aku ingin pergi ke sekolah lagi.

Dino: Mari berdoa semoga pandemi ini segera berakhir.

Dina: Apakah virus itu benar-benar berbahaya, Kak?

Dino: Iya, Dina. WHO bahkan menyatakan Covid-19 sebagai pandemi.

Dina: Benarkah? Makanya kita harus tinggal di rumah?

Dino: Ya. Karena kita mudah tertular jika melakukan kontak dengan orang lain di luar. Siapa tahu kalau seseorang terinfeksi? Itu sebabnya kita harus tinggal di rumah dan menghindari kontak langsung dengan orang-orang, kecuali keluarga kita di rumah.

Dina: Sedih sekali. Aku merindukan teman-temanku.

Dino: Ya, kakak juga merindukan teman-teman kakak. Tapi kita harus mematuhi aturan dan menjaga diri kita tetap aman.

Dina: Menurutmu, berapa lama sampai kita bisa hidup normal lagi?

Dino: Aku tidak tahu, Dina. Tidak ada yang bisa memastikan. Itulah kenapa kita tetap menjaga keamanan kita, supaya pandemi bisa cepat berakhir.

Dina: Oke. Ibu bilang kita juga harus olahraga dan makan makanan sehat, kan?

Dino: Benar! Kamu pintar, Dina.

24. diskripsikan tetang indonesia minimal 2 paragraf dengan bahasa inggris.


Borobudur temple is a very famous Budist temple. It is is one of the greatest Buddhist monuments in the world. Built in the 8th and 9th centuries by Syailendra Dynasty, the temple is consist of teen floors. It symbolize the teen floors of Bodhisattvas ( teen level to achive perfection ).

Borobudur Temple also can be divided into three levels. Those are Kamadatu, Rupadatu, and Arupadatu. Kamadatu symbolize the lowest level of human. Here people still tied to the law of karma. Here we can see Karma Wibangga Reliefs.

The second level is Rupadatu Level or the level of face. It symbolize people that starting to search for illumination. At this level, we can see Lalitavistara, the relief about Budha. There are also other reliefs and story at this level.

The last one is Arupadatu level or the level without face. It symbolize the perfection. It is the circle floors at the temple where we can see the many stupas.

40. Buatlah Sebuah 2 Paragraf Tetang Hari Kemerdekaan Dengan Bahasa Inggris​


Indonesia's independence day falls on August 17, Indonesia's independence is the most valuable moment for the Indonesian people. Indonesian independence was achieved with full struggle and bloodshed. We must commemorate Independence Day with joy and joy.
There are lots of activities carried out to commemorate independence day. One of them is by competition. I am very happy when I participate in competitions, both at school and in the village. The competition that I really enjoy doing is the tug of war, because tug of war trains us to work hard, cooperate and never give up. Like our heroes who work hard and never give up to gain independence.
However, not only on independence day we commemorate the services of heroes, but we must emulate the attitude of heroes in everyday life, for example, we must have the courage to uphold the truth, help each other, work together in the village, and be enthusiastic about learning as students.
